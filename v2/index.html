Before & after your session

What to know

Before your session

  • Eat a light meal 1–2 hours before. Don't arrive fasting or overly full.
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day beforehand.
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows access to your back, shoulders, or legs.
  • Avoid caffeine and alcohol for 24 hours before your appointment.
  • Bring any relevant health history or a medications list if you have one.

After your session

  • Rest for the remainder of the day. Avoid strenuous activity for 24 hours.
  • Drink plenty of water to support the body's natural process.
  • Circular marks are normal and typically fade within 3–10 days.
  • Keep cupped areas warm — avoid cold showers or cold wind on them for 24 hours.
  • Contact Khadijah if anything feels unexpected. She follows up after every session.

FAQ

Common questions

Does hijamah hurt?

Most clients describe it as a strong pressure or pulling sensation — not pain. Wet cupping involves a small, controlled incision that most clients barely feel. Khadijah checks in throughout the session and adjusts based on your comfort.

What are the circular marks after treatment?

The marks are caused by suction drawing blood to the surface. They're not bruises — they don't hurt when pressed — and they typically fade within 3 to 10 days. Darker marks generally indicate more stagnation in that area.

How many sessions will I need?

A single session provides real relief for most people. Chronic conditions benefit most from the Wellness Support Program (3 sessions). Khadijah will give you her honest assessment after your first session.

Is it safe?

Yes. As a Registered Nurse, Khadijah screens each client for contraindications before every session. Single-use sterile equipment is used every visit — nothing is reused between clients.

What is the deposit and cancellation policy?

A $35 deposit is required at booking and is applied to your session total. Cancellations at least 24 hours before your appointment receive a full deposit refund. Cancellations inside 24 hours forfeit the deposit.

Do you serve my neighborhood?

Khadijah serves Brooklyn and Queens, NY via mobile appointments. Your address is collected during booking — if you're outside the service area, you'll be notified before your deposit is charged.

Do I fill out a health form before I can book?

No. You book and pay the deposit first — intake comes after. Once your slot is confirmed, Khadijah will send a brief health history form by text or email well before your appointment.

Can I receive hijamah while pregnant?

Pregnant clients should consult their healthcare provider and inform Khadijah before booking. Certain modalities may not be appropriate during pregnancy.
