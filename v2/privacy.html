Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

Healing Hijamah ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you book or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Booking details — your name, phone number, email, and address (the address confirms you're within our mobile service area).
  • Health information — a brief health history you provide on the intake form after booking, used only to make your session safe and appropriate.
  • Payment information — your deposit is processed by our payment provider; we do not store full card numbers ourselves.

How we use your information

  • To schedule, confirm, and follow up on your appointment.
  • To screen for contraindications and personalize your treatment safely.
  • To send confirmations and reminders by text and email.
  • To respond to your questions and meet legal or accounting obligations.

How your information is shared

We do not sell your information. We share it only with the service providers that make booking work — our scheduling tool (Calendly) and payment processor (Stripe) — and only as needed to deliver your appointment, or when required by law. Your health information is kept confidential and is not shared for marketing.

Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide care, maintain records, and meet legal obligations, after which it is securely deleted.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and you can opt out of reminder messages at any time. To make a request, contact us at info@healinghijamah.com or (347) 687-4011.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach Khadijah at info@healinghijamah.com.
