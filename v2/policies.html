Policies

Last updated June 2026

Booking & deposit

A $35 deposit is collected when you book and is applied to your session total — it is not an extra charge. You book and pay the deposit first; a brief health-history intake form comes after your slot is confirmed. You never have to fill out paperwork before you can book.

Cancellations & rescheduling

  • Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment for a full deposit refund.
  • Cancellations or reschedules inside 24 hours forfeit the deposit.
  • No-shows forfeit the deposit.

If you need to make a change, call or text (347) 687-4011 as early as you can.

Before your session

  • Eat a light meal 1–2 hours before — don't arrive fasting or overly full.
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day beforehand.
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows access to your back, shoulders, or legs.
  • Avoid caffeine and alcohol for 24 hours before your appointment.
  • Bring any relevant health history or a current medications list.

After your session

  • Rest for the remainder of the day and avoid strenuous activity for 24 hours.
  • Drink plenty of water to support your body's natural process.
  • Circular marks are normal and typically fade within 3–10 days.
  • Keep cupped areas warm — avoid cold showers or cold wind on them for 24 hours.
  • Contact Khadijah if anything feels unexpected. She follows up after every session.

Service area & hours

Healing Hijamah is a mobile, women-only practice serving Brooklyn & Queens, NY — Khadijah comes to you. Your address is collected at booking to confirm you're within the service area before your deposit is charged. Hours are Tuesday–Friday 9:00 AM–6:00 PM and Saturday 9:00 AM–4:00 PM; closed Sunday and Monday.

Safety & suitability

As a Registered Nurse, Khadijah screens every client for contraindications before each session, and single-use sterile equipment is used on every visit. Pregnant clients should consult their healthcare provider and inform Khadijah before booking, as certain modalities may not be appropriate.

Medical disclaimer

Healing Hijamah provides complementary wellness services and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical advice.
