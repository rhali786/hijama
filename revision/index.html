Dry Cupping

Targeted suction therapy to relieve tightness, improve circulation, and support the body’s natural healing. Dry cupping is a straightforward and effective option for those seeking therapeutic benefits without the wet cupping process.

Dry cupping involves placing cups on specific areas of the body to create a controlled suction. This suction draws blood to the surface, helping to relieve muscle tightness, reduce discomfort, and improve circulation.
